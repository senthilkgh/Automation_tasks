Custom Command Analyzer

Paste output from any Linux command for instant pattern analysis. Works independently — no sosreport needed.

Suggested commands to paste:
ps aux  |  df -h  |  free -m  |  ss -tunapl  |  netstat -an  |  top -bn1  |  vmstat 1 5  |  cat /var/log/messages  |  dmesg  |  iostat -x  |  sar -u 1 5
Detects: disk full · high memory · load · error keywords · TCP states (TIME_WAIT/CLOSE_WAIT) · zombie processes · SELinux denials
